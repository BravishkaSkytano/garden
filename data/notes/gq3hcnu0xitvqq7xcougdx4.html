Magical Systems

Magic in Ilmaria is a fundamental part of life, with different regions having their own unique forms of magic:

  • Earth Magic in the Crystalline Peaks (Private) allows for the manipulation of minerals and gemstones.
  • Nature Magic in the Eldoria Forests (Private) connects users with the flora and fauna.
  • Wind Magic in the Shimmering Isles (Private) harnesses the power of air and flight.
  • Water Magic in the Sunken Abyss (Private) controls the seas and aquatic life.
  • Fire Magic in the Ashen Wastes (Private) commands the element of fire and heat.
